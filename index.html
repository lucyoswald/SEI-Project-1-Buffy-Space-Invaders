INSTRUCTIONS

This is a Buffy the Vampire Slayer styled Space Invaders game - (one of my favourite tv shows).

Angel has lost his soul again, and teamed up with Drusilla & Spike to try and kill Buffy.You will have to stake each Vampire if you want to save the world and defeat them!

  • You can move Buffy from left to right using ← or → arrow keys.
  • Throw stakes to kill Vampires using the space bar.
  • But be careful, if the Vampires get you, Buffy will die and it'll be GAME OVER!
  • If you successfully stake a Vampire you will gain 100 points!
  • Kill all the vampires and you win the game + 1000 extra points!
  • The Vampires will also occasionally throw fangs at you, remember if they bite you Buffy will die and it'll be GAME OVER!
